About Us

Solaris Harvest Innovations was founded in 2023 with the aim of harnessing renewable energy to optimize agricultural productivity. Our team is comprised of scientists, engineers, and farmers working together to push the boundaries of sustainable farming.

From vertical farming solutions to solar-powered irrigation systems, we integrate technology and nature to build resilient food systems.
